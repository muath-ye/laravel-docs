Painless local email testing for web developers.

HELO is a desktop application that will change the way you test your emails.
No more broken links, missing images, and customer complaints.

Download Trial Watch the video tour
The swiss-army-knife of
email testing and debugging.

Debug Views

HELO exposes an SMTP server and catches the outgoing emails from your application instead of sending them to their actual recipient.

Broken Link Checker

The automated link check verifies that all links in an email are valid and return an HTTP 200 status code. Don't send emails with broken links again.

Spam Report

If activated, HELO automatically checks the Apache SpamAssassin score of your incoming emails. Never lose a mail in your users spam box again.

Sharing

Share multiple emails with all the information that you see in HELO. Test all emails of your application, and share them with your client easily.

Remote Mailboxes

HELO allows you to create publicly available email
addresses. Send any email to those addresses,
and they will appear inside the HELO app.

Frequently asked
questions

What are concurrent activations?

One activation is connected to one machine. You can remove an activation to activate it on a different machine anytime.

What is HELO cloud?

HELO cloud is the service that you interact with, when you share your local emails or use remote mailboxes.
