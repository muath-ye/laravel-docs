About

The largest Laravel event of the year
streaming free on YouTube.

For the first time ever we'll be streaming Laracon for free on YouTube allowing us to reach the entire Laravel community. This is a huge moment (and experiment) for us. Thank you to our incredible partners for helping us achieve this!

All talks are brand new, never before presented at any Laracon, and presented live during the event. We have 17 amazing speakers lined up including a keynote from Taylor Otwell on the state of Laravel and what's new in Laravel 9.

Watch it now

Can't watch it ●LIVE?

No problem, all talks will be recorded and available online for viewing at your convenience shortly after the conference ends.
