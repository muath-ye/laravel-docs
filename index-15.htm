The Venue

Monkey Baa is Australia's leading professional theatre company for young audiences, situated a few minutes’ walk from Darling Harbour.

Based in Sydney's Darling Quarter, the conference venue is surrounded by a diverse range of eateries and shops, with plenty of room to enjoy Sydney's temperate Spring climate.

After Dark

Kingpin Darling Harbour sits pretty on waterfront Darling Harbour, just a few minutes from Monkey Baa.

Team up with your fellow Artisans in a 40-person laser tag arena, bowl head to head across 20 lanes in four zones, enjoy table tennis and pool, throw down in the arcade, or belt out your favourite karaoke tunes.
