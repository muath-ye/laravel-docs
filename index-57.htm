Webhook testing

Webhooks require a publicly accessible endpoint for the 3rd party application – use Expose and receive the payload on your local machine. Inspect and replay the request until you are ready to deploy.

Collaboration

Tired of screensharing? Share your local sites and applications with colleagues via public URLs. Quickly get feedback from others without the hassle of deploying minor changes of your application to a server.
